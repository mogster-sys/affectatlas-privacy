AffectAtlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Summary

AffectAtlas does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

AffectAtlas does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers. No data is sent to any server.

Local Storage

The app stores your most recent emotion selection locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.

Third-Party Services

AffectAtlas loads Google Fonts for typography display. Google's font service may log standard web request data (IP address, browser type). See Google Fonts Privacy for details.

Network Access

The app requires internet permission solely for loading web fonts. No user data is transmitted.

Children's Privacy

AffectAtlas does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact mogster@threeeyedemu.com.au.
